WONDERS OF KOTTAYAM

Kottayam, is a city in the Indian state of Kerala. Flanked by the Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake
and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west. Kottayam is located in the basin of the Meenachil River at an average
elevation of 3 metres (9.8 ft) above sea level, and has a moderate climate. It is located approximately
155 kilometres (96 mi) north of the state capital Thiruvananthapuram.

Kottayam is a wonderful district in Central Kerala bordered by the lofty Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad
Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west. Kuttanad is blessed with serene backwater stretches, lush paddy fields,
hills and hillocks, rubber plantations and many more attractions.
