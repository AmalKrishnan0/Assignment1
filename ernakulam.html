WONDERS OF ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam, is a district of Kerala, India, that takes its name from the eponymous city division in Kochi. It is situated
in the central part of the state, spans an area of about 3,000 square kilometres (1,200 sq mi), and is home to over
9% of Kerala's population.

With one leg in the past, and other in the future, Ernakulam is a heady mix of modern, and ancient tourist attractions.
