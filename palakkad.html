WONDERS OF PALAKKAD

Palakkad District, in the southeastern part of the former Malabar district, is one of the 14 districts of the Indian state
of Kerala. It is located right in the middle of the state. Also, it is the largest district in Kerala since 2006.

Palakkad district has a large number of temples, churches, mosques and other tourist attractions. Palakkad district is situated
in the middle of Kerala state in South India. The nearest airports are at Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu and Cochin in Kerala.
