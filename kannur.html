WONDERS OF KANNUR

Kannur is one of the 14 districts along the west coast in the state of Kerala, India.Kannur is the sixth-most urbanised district
in Kerala, with more than 50% of its residents living in urban areas. Kannur has an urban population of 1,640,986,
which is the second largest in Kerala after Ernakulam district. It was part of the Malabar District under the
Madras province during the British Raj.

Kannur, India has a number of tourist attractions including beaches, hills, temples and other monuments.
