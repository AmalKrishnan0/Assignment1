WONDERS OF KASARAGOD

Kasaragod is the northernmost district of Kerala and is also known as Saptha Bhasha Sangama Bhoomi
(The land of seven languages) as seven languages namely, Malayalam, Tulu, Kannada, Marathi, Konkani, Beary,
and Urdu are spoken, unlike the other districts of Kerala

Kasaragod district is endowed with 9 rivers (out of a total of the 44 rivers that flow in Kerala), hills, beaches, backwaters,
as well as temples, churches, mosques and forts.
