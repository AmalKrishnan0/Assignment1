WONDERS OF KOLLAM

Kollam district (formerly Quilon) is one of 14 districts of the state of Kerala, India. The district has a cross-section of
Kerala's natural attributes

It is endowed with a long coastline, a major Laccadive Sea seaport and an inland lake (Ashtamudi Lake). The district
has many water bodies. Kallada River is one among them, and the east side land of river is East Kallada and the west
side land is West Kallada.
